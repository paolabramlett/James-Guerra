About Us

At Wealth Plan Group we hold the same fiduciary mentality that is central to our wealth strategies. Tragically, many salespeople in our industry are more concerned about selling a product than solving a problem. We are, comprehensively across every discipline, focused relentlessly on your long-term financial goals. We will only recommend those products that can provide a solution to an identifiable problem. Additionally, we are not affiliated with any one financial institute and therefore we are able to find the most effective solution for each client’s unique circumstances. Once we have identified your needs through developing a comprehensive financial plan, we are able to match your needs with the best available financial institute and products, without bias.


We look at each company’s rating, cost versus benefit analysis, and customer service when deciding which plan to recommend. By contrast, many agents are exclusively affiliated with a single provider, thereby restricting the agent’s ability to make comprehensive recommendations.
